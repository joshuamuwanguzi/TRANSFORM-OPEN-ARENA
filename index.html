AboutWho We are

Transform Open Arena is a vibrant Ugandan platform dedicated to nurturing the talents and skills of young individuals, focusing on debates, public speaking, poetry, and leadership development.

It's a dynamic space nurturing youth to express themselves and lead. Encouraging exploration of diverse perspectives, honing communication, and fostering leadership through engaging activities. Embracing curiosity and dialogue, empowering the next generation with resilience and insight. A transformative setting where potential is realized, shaping tomorrow's impactful leaders.


This initiative, launched in early 2023, strives to nurture socially conscious and confident individuals, fostering self-expression and empowerment. It offers a vibrant platform for personal development, encouraging growth within a supportive community.

Vision & VisionOur Mission & Vision

Our Vision

TOA envisions cultivating articulate Productive leaders proficient in critical thinking, Problem solving, empowering them to contribute to generational success.

Our Mission

We are committed to connect, support, and inspire a diverse community of young people committed to be the agents of positive change here in Uganda, Africa and the entire world.

ourhistory

We are now making 2 years in the journey of Constant Commitment!

Transform Open Arena was created in late 2022 with the aim of bridging Gaps in students capacity Development. A group of Form 6 vacists then after engaging into a program “VACIST SHOW” that was initiated by Paul Achaye to engage Vacists who were in holidays with meaningful connections, found it worth to start an impactful platform for every young person to have their talents and skills appreciated.

The team was Led by Dickens Opio Agona with immediate support of Mary Sharon Karungi.To appreciate Our History, Names like Odong Robben, Husnah Babirye Kayaga and Namata June made a great team in the establishment.

Wall Of Impacts And Memories

01.

TOA has held 2 successful Schools Events. TOA 0.1 online Edition Of debates & Public Speech in 2023 that showed about 600+ students at high school participate online. Beyond the championship, students were able to have colorful conversations with influential Figures like Mr. TOM ETII of Uganda Aids Commission, Mercy Melody Kayodi of UNDP among many other Influential acts.

TOA 2.0 CHRISTMAS edition was the second edition of our event that happened online with much impacts. Numbers of Participants raised to 1000+ from Uganda and other foreign Countries Like Qatar, South Africa, India, Ghana, Nigeria, Rwanda, Kenya, Zimbabwe and may other Countries. In the History of Debates in Uganda, this became the most successful Online Tournament. The tournament attracted Colorful capacity building by Top recognized figures in the circuit like Nkosilathi Nkala (Zimbabwe National debate team assistant Coach), Coach Methembe Mthimkhulu (COO of Royalty pact Debate academy) among many others
